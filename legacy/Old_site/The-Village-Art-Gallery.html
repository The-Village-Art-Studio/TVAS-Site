Showcase your art in different dimension

The Village Art Gallery is a virtual 3D art gallery operated by The Village Art Studio. 
The project aims to support local artists and exhibit their work to a global audience through an immersive experience beyond a traditional physical art gallery. 
Additionally, we collaborate with businesses to host events to drive traffic and increase exposure for artists. 

Let's join us on this journey.

JUMP IN 

*Decentraland is a 3D virtual world platform. Users may buy virtual plots of land in the platform as NFTs via the MANA cryptocurrency, which uses the Ethereum blockchain. Designers can create and sell clothes and accessories for the avatars to be used in the virtual world.

Interested in showcasing your art here?

In this project, we are prioritizing our TVAS artist members first. You can click the link to check out our TVAS Artist Program to get more information.

If you are already a TVAS artist member, please make sure you have everything ready below.

1. ​Artist Statement

  • A picture of you
  • A biography in the third person. Represent you as an artist, even while you are not there.

2. Scanned Artwork - 2 pieces at least

  • Each has to be 300 dpi in JPG or PNG format
  • Title, years, medium, physical size (inch)

3. Links

  • Link to your porfolio
  • Links to your social media
