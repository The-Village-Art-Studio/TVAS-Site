What Is Art?

Dec 03, 2023UncategorizedComments (0)

Did you ever grab a crayon and begin doodling, or snap a picture of a sunset because it was just right? Well, guess what? That’s art!

We at The Village Art Studio feel that there’s more to art than strokes of paint upon a canvas. Art is a means of self-expression, storytelling, and viewing the world in a different light. Whether age 5 or age 95, art can be enjoyed by everyone and experienced by all.


So, what exactly is art?

Anything we make to convey ideas, emotion, or imagination is art. It can be a drawing, a painting, sculpting, photographing, a dance, an acting performance, or designing a video game character. If you make it from your heart in a way that tells a story or expresses an emotion, then it’s art.

Some create art to unwind and have a good time. Some create to express themselves, to express what they feel inside, or to make people think. There isn’t any right or wrong way to create art. That’s the magic in it—each piece of art is special, as unique as you.


Why does art matter?

Art develops us. It instructs us to observe closely, think intensively, and perceive beauty in everyday objects. It also teaches us to understand ourselves and others better. And here’s a surprise! You don’t have to be a “professional” to create art. Just have curiosity and a bit of creativity.

We at The Village Art Studio enjoy assisting individuals in uncovering their built-in creativity. Whether you’re a little child new to holding a paintbrush, a teen discovering your style, or a grown-up new to it all—our doors are open to you.


Come Create With Us!

We have workshops, camps, and classes for all ages. Our studio is a space to experiment, get messy, and most importantly—have fun. So go ahead, next time you question whether or not you can make art, keep in mind that if it’s from you, it’s already unique.

🎨 Art belongs to everyone. Art belongs to YOU.
